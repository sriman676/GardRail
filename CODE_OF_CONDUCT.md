# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

Our community is dedicated to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone. Please read and follow the full Code of Conduct below.

Standards
- Be respectful and considerate.
- Refrain from demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behavior.

Enforcement
- Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers who will take appropriate action.

For the full text and reporting details, see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
