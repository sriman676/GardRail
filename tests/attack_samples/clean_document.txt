SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement is entered into as of January 1, 2024, between Acme Corporation
("Client") and Beta Consulting Ltd ("Provider").

1. SERVICES: Provider agrees to deliver strategic advisory services including monthly
   reporting, quarterly business reviews, and ad-hoc consulting as requested.

2. TERM: This agreement shall commence January 1, 2024 and terminate December 31, 2024
   unless extended by mutual written consent.

3. PAYMENT: Client shall pay Provider $10,000 USD per month, due on the first business
   day of each month. Late payments accrue 1.5% monthly interest.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY: Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding
   each other's proprietary information for a period of 3 years post-termination.

5. TERMINATION: Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.

Signed and agreed by authorized representatives of both parties on January 1, 2024.
